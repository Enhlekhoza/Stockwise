# StockWise: AI-Powered Retail Management

*   **Project Title:** StockWise
*   **Short Description:** An AI-powered inventory and business management system designed to empower small shop owners with intelligent tools for efficient operation, loss prevention, and data-driven decision-making.
*   **Long Description:** StockWise provides small retail businesses, particularly those in informal settings, with a comprehensive suite of AI-driven functionalities. It streamlines daily operations through an automated point-of-sale, minimizes losses via an AI security guard, optimizes stock levels with an autonomous supply chain manager, and offers strategic insights through a generative business advisor. The system aims to enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and improve profitability by transforming complex business challenges into manageable, AI-assisted workflows.

*   **Lack of Data-Driven Insights:** Manual processes and limited access to analytics hinder informed decision-making.
*   **Complex Supply Chain Logistics:** Difficulty in accurately forecasting demand and managing supplier relationships.

*   **Autonomous Supply Chain Manager:** Predictive demand forecasting, smart supplier management, and automated purchase order generation.
*   **Generative Business Advisor:** Conversational AI providing tailored business insights and strategic recommendations.
